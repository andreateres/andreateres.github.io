Guide to SKAM España

What is SKAM España?

  • SKAM España is a Spanish teen drama television series based on the Norwegian television series 'SKAM.'
  • Its four seasons originally aired from September 2018 to October 2020 on Movistar+
  • Currently there exist seven remakes of the original series, including the Spain remake.
  • Each remake follows the same characters, though the conflicts they face are shaped by each country's culture.
  • Each season of any SKAM remake follows a different character.

Season Four: Amira

Available on Movistar+

SKAM's most recent season follows the character Amira, who struggles
with balancing school, friends, love, and her devotion to Islam as a practicing muslim.

Season 4 is the fourth and the last season of Skam España. Season 4 was going to air on April 9, 2020,
but it was postponed due to the coronavirus pandemic.

Season Two: Cris

Available on Movistar+

Season 2 of SKAM was praised by fans for the way it dealt with heavy topics such as infidelity, mental health,
sexuality, religion and sexual assault.

The second season follows the character Cris, and her inability to deal with serious problems.
